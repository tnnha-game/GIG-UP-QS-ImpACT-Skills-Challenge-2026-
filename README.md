# GIG-UP: A 2D Systems-Based Simulation Game

## 🚀 Overview
GIG-UP: Don’t Just Survive, Thrive!

Welcome to GIG-UP, a 2D systems-based simulation game developed by team Vinexus from VinUniversity for the QS ImpACT Skills Challenge 2026.

💼 The Challenge: Step into the shoes of Alex, a young person navigating the volatile world of the rise of the Gig Economy and the rapid evolution of AI. The gig economy presents high volatility and structural uncertainty, where individuals often trade long-term health for short-term survival. Your mission? Balance 5 core life stats: Cash, Energy, Health, Stress, and Skill in a 90-day life simulation. 

🌱 The Impact: Through immersive gameplay, GIG-UP models labor market instability and the critical need for reskilling. By navigating Alex’s journey, players directly engage with core SDGs 1, 3, 4, 8, 10, and 16, toward socioeconomic development. This game focuses on tackling financial precariousness and the drive for equitable economic growth, emphasizing lifelong learning, protecting good health & mental well-being, and the ethical resilience needed to resist social temptations and illegal shortcuts in the face of extreme hardship. Can you help Alex rise above the bills, build a sustainable career, and find a balanced life?

Every choice is a transaction. Every click defines your future.
